Hello, I'm Siddhi, a freelance creative based in Mumbai, India. I'm a versatile creator with diverse passions spanning fashion, advertising, and user experience design.

I love crafting authentic and engaging creatives that tell meaningful stories, connecting people and uncovering beauty in everyday moments.

Within me coexist two forces: the logical and the creative. Currently, I'm pursuing a bachelor's degree in communication design while freelancing in design, a combination that sparks fresh, thoughtful, and genuinely compelling ideas.

When I'm not immersed in work for hours on end, you'll often find me traveling, attending talks, thrifting, discovering hidden gems, or enjoying time with friends. Ultimately, my goal is to create work that feels inviting, warm, and evokes nostalgia.
