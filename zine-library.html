Zine Library

This project is a curated library of experimental zines created to explore the possibilities of print as a medium. Each zine reflects a distinct theme, narrative voice, and visual identity, allowing me to push the boundaries of layout, typography, and image-making. Working across ideas—from imagination and nostalgia to subcultures and urban life—I used the zine format as a space for creative freedom and self-expression.
